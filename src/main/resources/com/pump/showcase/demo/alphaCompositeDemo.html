AlphaComposite Demo

What Is It

This demo shows how different java.awt.AlphaComposites render.

On the rare occasion that I need a composite other than SRC_OVER: sometimes I forget exactly which composite I need. And the AlphaComposite javadoc is great, but the mathematical expression isn't as useful (to me) as just seeing the composites in action.

Most of the demos in this application showcase a novel new piece of code I want to share. But this isn't of those demos. This is just a simple helper class that let's you visually experiment with AlphaComposites.

How To Use It

Observe how changing the controls in the "Configuration" panel affect the "Example" panel.
